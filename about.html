About the Podcast

This podcast focuses on human interaction with the natural world. First, we address monarch butterfly decline and endangerment, which is representative of the global biodiversity crisis. Next, this topic is covered from a broader perspective, diving into the ways our choices and actions have an impact on our surroundings. We also explore the career of environmental law. Finally, we touch on how research is a valuable way to gain knowledge, and English 105 students reflect on their experiences disseminating information in a podcast format. About the Podcaster

Hi, as you may know, I am Katie Jorczak. Thank you again for stopping by. I wanted to take the time to share a little bit more about myself and give all of my listeners the opportunity to get to know me a little bit better.

I was born Torrance, California but currently live in Palos Verdes, California. I spent all most of my life there until it came time to go to college. I decided to try something new and go to school on the east coast. The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill gave me the opportunity to do just that. I am currently pursuing pre-business at UNC but I have a strong interest in environmental studies.
